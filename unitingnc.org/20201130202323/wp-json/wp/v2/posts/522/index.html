Uniting NC has created a series of video interviews that tell the stories of immigrants in North Carolina. Today, we posted our newest.<\/p>\n

Saleem’s story shows the complex paths that lead people from all over the world to our state, and it challenges the notion that there is a stark divide between immigrants and natives.<\/p>\n

Saleem was born in the United States and grew up in North Carolina. But his father is from India. His mother is the child of a Japanese woman and an American serviceman. His family is Muslim.<\/p>\n

Please listen to his story and tell us what you think.<\/p>\n
