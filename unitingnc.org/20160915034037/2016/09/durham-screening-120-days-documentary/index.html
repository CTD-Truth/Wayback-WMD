Durham screening of ‘120 Days’ documentary

September 14, 2016 By Grace Martien Leave a Comment

In 2013 a family in North Carolina faced a monumental decision when the father/husband Miguel Cortes was asked to leave the country in 120 days after driving without a license. The  documentary 120 Days follows their decision whether to stay in North Carolina or return to Mexico — providing unique insight into the difficult decisions facing many undocumented immigrant families living in the United States.

Please join Uniting NC for our screening on September 29th at 7:00pm in the Durham Academy Auditorium! The director of 120 Days, Ted Roach, will join for a Q&A after the film. More details here.

Address Durham Academy, 3601 Ridge Road, Durham, NC 27705-5541
