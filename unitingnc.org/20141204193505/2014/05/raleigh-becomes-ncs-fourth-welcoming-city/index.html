Raleigh becomes NC’s fourth Welcoming City

May 25, 2014 By Dan Rearick Leave a Comment

Last July, Uniting NC made a presentation to the Raleigh Human Relations Commission asking the city to recognize and support the contributions of new immigrants to our community. Yesterday Raleigh officially became a Welcoming City, and we look forward to continuing to work with the city and our partners at Welcoming America to make it an even better place to live for all of us.
